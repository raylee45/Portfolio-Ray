Some Cool Facts About Me

My background in pre-medicine has honed my ability to understand user pain points and build rapport with others efficiently, which informs my empathetic approach to software development. I thrive to find an environment and company where I can share my knowledge and growth, while also expanding them with others as we all innovate and inspire each other.
